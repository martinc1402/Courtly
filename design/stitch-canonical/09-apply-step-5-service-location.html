ABOUT SERVICE AREAS

Service areas are the parts of the city where you're available — for incall, outcall, or both. They appear on your public profile as a list (e.g. 'Available in Sablon · Brussels Centre · Ixelles') and as fuzzy radius circles on the directory map.

  • check_circleYour exact address is never shown publicly
  • check_circleMap circles are deliberately fuzzy and randomised
  • check_circleCapped at 5 to keep results meaningful for clients
  • check_circleYou can update your service areas any time from your dashboard

MODALITY, IN PLAIN WORDS

Incall fixed

Clients come to a place where you're set up — your own apartment, studio, or regular room.

Incall rotating

You take rooms in different hotels or venues. Useful if you don't want a fixed address tied to your work.

Outcall

You travel to the client's location — usually a hotel or their home.

Most providers offer one or two of these. Pick honestly — clients value clarity.

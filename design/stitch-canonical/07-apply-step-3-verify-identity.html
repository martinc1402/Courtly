HOW WE HANDLE YOUR DOCUMENTS

Documents are reviewed by a small Courtly team — usually within 3–5 working days. We store the minimum needed to record your verification status (date and reviewer initials). We do not store the raw ID image once verification is complete unless required by Belgian law.

  • check_circle Reviewed by a human, not an algorithm
  • check_circle Never shown publicly on your profile
  • check_circle Stored only as long as legally required

WHAT WE LOOK FOR

  • ID confirms you are 18 or older
  • ID photo matches your selfie
  • Handwritten note is legible and includes today's date
  • Selfie matches your profile photos

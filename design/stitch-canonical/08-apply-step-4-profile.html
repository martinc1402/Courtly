Tips for a strong profile

  • 1. Write in the first person. "I enjoy..." feels more personal than "She enjoys..."
  • 2. Be specific about your interests. "Art" is broad; "Belgian surrealism" is a conversation starter.
  • 3. Quality over quantity. A few well-crafted paragraphs are better than a long, generic list.

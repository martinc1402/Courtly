About

I'm an art historian by training and by inclination — I work freelance with smaller Belgian galleries and spend more time than I should in the Magritte and the Bozar.

What I bring to the time we spend together is patient attention. I'm a good listener, I read widely, and I think company should feel unhurried — long lunches, quiet hotel bars, the kind of evening where conversation finds its own rhythm without anyone needing to perform.

I speak French, English, and Dutch. I'm most comfortable in Sablon, the Centre, and Ixelles. I welcome thoughtful clients who value good company as much as good conversation, and I prefer that first meetings happen in public — a coffee, a drink — so we can both get a sense of each other before anything longer.
